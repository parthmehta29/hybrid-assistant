Company Travel Policy (2025 Update)

1. Flight Bookings
- All domestic flights under 4 hours must be Economy Class.
- International flights over 6 hours are eligible for Premium Economy.
- Business Class is only approved for executive leadership (VP and above) or flights exceeding 12 hours.
- Advance booking of at least 14 days is required for all non-emergency travel.

2. Hotel Accommodation
- Maximum nightly rate: $200 (Tier 2 cities), $350 (Tier 1 cities: NYC, SF, London, Tokyo).
- Employees must use the corporate portal (TravelPerk) for all bookings.
- Airbnb is permitted only for stays longer than 7 days, with manager approval.

3. Expense Reimbursement
- Daily meal allowance (Per Diem) is $75.
- Receipts are required for any single expense over $25.
- Alcohol is not reimbursable unless part of a client dinner (pre-approval required).

4. Remote Work & equipment
- One-time home office stipend of $500 for new hires.
- Company laptops must be used for all work; personal devices are strictly prohibited for code access.
